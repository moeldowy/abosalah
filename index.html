About Me

As a Senior Software Engineer with over 15 years of experience, I have a proven track record of delivering high-quality web applications and digital solutions. My expertise lies in developing robust and scalable web applications using PHP, Laravel, Restful Architecture, REST APIs, and WordPress. I am highly proficient in HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, TailWind, and JavaScript, and have some experience with Angular and React frameworks. I have a deep understanding of software development principles and can write clean, maintainable, and reusable code using OOP concepts. I am well-versed in database management systems like MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MS SQL Server, and can design and implement efficient database schemas. I have experience working with various VCS tools like Git and have hands-on experience with GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket. My expertise in Laravel and WordPress allows me to develop custom themes and plugins that meet specific client requirements. I have a keen eye for design and can create visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that improve the overall user experience. Overall, I am a dedicated and detail-oriented developer who is passionate about creating innovative solutions that drive business growth.
